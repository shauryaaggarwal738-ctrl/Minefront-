⚔️ Announcement — Trial Tier Testers (Java & Bedrock Divisions)

We welcome all newly appointed Trial Tier Testers for both Java and Bedrock divisions. All testers begin with Tier 1 (Trial Phase). Fully understand and apply these rules before assigning any tier.

📊 SCORING SYSTEM
10START SCORE
−1PER MISTAKE
↓MORE = LOWER
⚠️ If Seriousness < 5 → Test is INVALID (Retake required)
🔍 WHAT WE CHECK
01
SERIOUSNESS
  • Stay focused throughout
  • No trolling, no excuses
02
GAME SENSE
  • Smart decision-making under pressure
  • Ability to adapt mid-fight
03
REACTION SPEED
  • Fast combos, flicks, counters
04
GAME MODE SKILL
  • Must match the claimed tier level
05
MECHANICS
  • Proper hit selecting & jump reset — no spam
  • Fast and controlled keybind usage
06
MOVEMENT
  • Unpredictable — not only W-key
  • Good strafing
07
TIMING
  • Correct timing on hits, blocks, bridging
08
AIM & CONTROL
  • Accurate and consistent aim
  • No excuses
09
STRATEGY & MENTAL
  • Effective tactics
  • Calm gameplay — no panic
10
OVERALL PERFORMANCE
  • Total mistakes (−) vs good plays (+)
  • Consistency matters most
✅ CORE RULES
✔ Less mistakes = Higher tier
✔ More consistency = Better player
✔ Serious gameplay = Valid result

All trial testers must strictly follow these rules during Tier 1 testing before assigning any results.

— Head of Tier Testing, Chief of Tier Testing
